Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Craft MadeBy (“we”, “us”) collects, how we use it, and your choices. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

Account information you provide: email address, display name, and (optionally) a profile photo and bio. If you sign in with Google, we receive your basic Google profile (name, email, avatar).

Content you create: projects, photos, steps, materials, techniques, comments, reactions, groups, and giveaway details.

Technical data: a device push-notification token, app language, and basic diagnostics/crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics) to keep the app stable.

How we use it

To provide the app: create your account, show your projects and feeds, deliver notifications, and run features like the Expert Panel and giveaways. To keep the community safe (moderation and handling reports). To improve reliability and fix crashes. We do not sell your personal data.

Service providers

We use trusted processors to run the app: Supabase (database, authentication, storage), Google Firebase (push notifications, crash reporting), Google Sign-In, and our email provider for account emails. Payments, when available, are processed by Apple/Google and RevenueCat — we never receive your card details.

Data security & retention

Data is encrypted in transit. We keep your information while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account, which permanently removes your profile and content.

Your choices

You can edit your profile, turn push notifications off, request your data, or delete your account. To exercise any privacy right, contact us at support@craftmadeby.com.

Children

Craft MadeBy is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy; significant changes will be reflected by the date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
