Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 2026

This page explains how to delete your Craft MadeBy account (developer: Craft MadeBy) and what happens to your data.

Delete your account from inside the app

The fastest way — your account is deleted immediately:

  1. Open the Craft MadeBy app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to the Account / Danger zone section and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and content are permanently removed.

Request deletion by email

If you can no longer sign in, email us from your registered email address at support@craftmadeby.com with the subject “Delete my account”. We will verify your identity and delete your account within 30 days.

What data is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your login and profile (name, email, photo, bio).
  • Your projects, photos, steps, materials, and techniques.
  • Your connections, group memberships, reactions, comments, and giveaway requests.
  • Your notifications and device push-notification tokens.

What may be kept, and for how long

After deletion, residual copies may remain only in encrypted backups for a limited period and are automatically purged within 30 days. We may retain a minimal record where required by law (for example, transaction/tax records related to any purchases). Purchase history is also held by Apple/Google and RevenueCat under their own policies.

Need help?

Contact us at support@craftmadeby.com and we’ll assist.
