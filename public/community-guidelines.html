Community Guidelines

Last updated: June 2026

Craft MadeBy is a place to celebrate handmade craft. Keep it kind, safe, and respectful. Breaking these guidelines can lead to content removal or account suspension.

Be respectful

Be encouraging. No harassment, bullying, hate speech, threats, or attacks based on who someone is. Critique craft, not people.

Respect copyright

Share your own makes and your own process. Do not post or redistribute other people’s patterns, recipes, or instructions — including paid patterns from designers and brands (e.g. PetiteKnit and similar). Showing your finished piece is welcome; copying a designer’s written pattern into your steps is not. Credit designers where you can.

Stay safe around strangers

Treat other members as strangers. Never share personal details — home address, phone number, financial information, or precise location. For giveaways, arrange exchanges carefully, prefer shipping over in-person meetups, and never send money or sensitive info. We do not verify member identities.

Keep it clean & legal

No sexual, violent, graphic, or illegal content. No promotion of dangerous or illegal activities. No spam, scams, or misleading content.

No impersonation

Don’t pretend to be someone else or misrepresent your work.

Report & block

If you see something wrong, use Report on the project or profile, and Block users you don’t want to interact with. Reports are reviewed and acted on. Thank you for helping keep the community safe.
